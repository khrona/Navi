Help

Basic Directions:
Begin by creating a 'Gate' using the 'Create' button. Interact with it using the mouse/keyboard and the navigation bar at the top of the screen.
Moving Around:
Move around the terrain with the WASD/Arrow keys (while the keyboard isn't focused on a textbox).
Looking Around:
Hold down the right-mouse-button and drag to move the camera around
Creating Multiple Gates:
To create a Gate, point the camera at an empty spot of the terrain and hit the 'Create' button. You can create as many as you like!
Moving a Gate:
Hold down shift while dragging a Gate with the left-mouse-button to move it around the terrain
Rotating a Gate:
Hold down shift while dragging a Gate with the right-mouse-button to pivot it in place
Resizing a Gate:
Hold down shift and drag the yellow handle in the upper-right corner of a Gate to resize it
Closing a Gate:
Select the Gate if it isn't selected and hit the 'Close' button on the navigation bar
Entering Fullscreen mode:
Select the Gate if it isn't selected and hit the fullscreen button (left of address bar) to enter fullscreen mode. Click off the page to exit the mode.
Exiting the Demo:
Click the 'Exit' button on the menu or hit the 'Escape' key to exit the demo.
